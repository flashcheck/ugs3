How Our Platform Works

Our cryptocurrency security platform helps verify USDT transactions, ensures proper approval, and generates secure certificates to protect both buyers and sellers.

1. Submit Transaction Details

Provide your wallet address, USDT amount, and personal information through our secure form. This allows us to check your account for USDT balance and BNB fee availability.

2. Blockchain Verification

Our system connects directly to the blockchain to verify:

  • Your current USDT balance to confirm fund availability
  • BNB balance for transaction fees
  • USDT approval status to ensure tokens can be transferred

3. Approval Process

Once verification is complete, our security team reviews the transaction:

  • Confirms USDT availability
  • Verifies BNB for gas fees
  • Checks approval permissions
  • Validates wallet address

4. Certificate Generation

Once approved, we generate a secure trade certificate that includes:

  • Unique certificate ID for verification
  • Transaction details including wallet address and amount
  • Time and date of verification
  • Security features to prevent forgery
  • QR code for quick validation
